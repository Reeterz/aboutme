About Myself

As a BSIT (Bachelor of Science in Information Technology) student, I am passionate about exploring the dynamic world of technology. My journey as an average person with an innate curiosity has led me to pursue this field, where I find endless opportunities to learn and grow.


In the realm of information technology, I am fascinated by how technology permeates every aspect of our lives, shaping the way we communicate, work, and interact with the world around us. From coding and software development to networking and cybersecurity, I am eager to delve into various domains within IT to broaden my knowledge and skills.


Outside of academics, I enjoy engaging in activities that help me unwind and recharge. Whether it's spending time with friends and family, indulging in a good book, or pursuing a hobby like photography or gaming, I believe in maintaining a healthy balance between work and leisure.


While I may consider myself an average person, I am driven by a desire to make a positive impact in the world, leveraging the power of technology to solve problems and create innovative solutions. As I navigate through my BSIT program, my goal is not only to acquire technical expertise but also to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills that will serve me well in my future endeavors.


Overall, I embrace my identity as a BSIT student and an average person with a passion for technology, eager to embark on a journey of discovery and personal growth.


My Life Story


Born in the lively streets of Sta. Cruz, Manila, I now find myself residing in the tranquil embrace of Mindanao, within the nurturing walls of my grandparents' home. From the urban hustle to the rural calm, my journey reflects the contrast of two worlds. Despite the distance, the values instilled in me amidst the bustling city remain steadfast resilience, perseverance, and a deep appreciation for simplicity.


Here in Mindanao, amidst the verdant landscapes and the gentle rhythm of rural life, I find solace and connection. Surrounded by the love and wisdom of my grandparents, I am reminded of the importance of family, heritage, and the enduring spirit of home. In this convergence of past and present, I continue to cherish the richness of my Manila roots while embracing the serenity of my newfound haven in Mindanao.
